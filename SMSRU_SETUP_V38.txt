SMS.ru setup for v38

Required Cloudflare variables:
SMS_PROVIDER=smsru
SMSRU_API_ID=your_api_id
NOCODB_SMS_ENDPOINT=your_NocoDB_SMS_queue_records_endpoint
SMS_CRON_SECRET=SOLNCANET_SMS_CRON_2026_7B2E9C

Do NOT set SMSRU_SENDER. Leave it empty or delete it.
The site intentionally does not pass the SMS.ru `from` parameter. SMS.ru will choose the available default sender.
